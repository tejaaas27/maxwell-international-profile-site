About Us

MaxWell International
Pet Care

This is our first branch in Pune, the concept came into existence in 2018 when a dream was seen to build a beautiful place for the bundle of love in your life.....your four legged baby.Our decision to start a pet services venture was fueled by the recognition of the growing need for high-quality pet care services.

By combining our passion for the pets with a commitment to excellence, We strive to offer a reliable and exceptional solution for pet owners seeking the best for their furry friends.

We urge you to visit us once to see who we really are, our aim is to ensure that owners can go away safe in the knowledge that their pets are being loved and cared.
