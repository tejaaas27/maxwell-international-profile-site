Our
Mission

At Maxwell International Pet Care, our mission is simple yet profound to be the beacon of exceptional pet care. Going above and beyond grooming and boarding, we strive to create a haven for pets, fostering an environment where they thrive physically, emotionally, and socially.

Our Core Values

A happy, motivated and professionally trained and qualified team with a genuine passion for pets.

We highly value our relationship with all our customers, creating a business that provides an exceptionally high quality service and level of personal care for your pet.

We are committed to provide an exceptionally high-quality service, ensuring that every pet receives the best possible care.

We offer a level of personal care for your pets that goes above and beyond, treating every pet as if they were our own.

"At Maxwell International Pet Care our tight-knit team is fueled by a collective passion for pets. From our skilled groomers and caring pet sitters to our friendly customer service representatives, each team member contributes a unique expertise to create a seamless and positive experience for both pets and their owners. We take pride in our collaborative spirit, attention to detail, and unwavering commitments to provide top-notch pet services. At the core of our mission is a shared dedication to ensuring every pet feels loved, secured and happy in our care."
